## Design Principles (LOCKED)

1. **Slow**: Idle animation period 8-16s, never faster
2. **Subtle**: Main visual opacity 0.08-0.28, hover 0.22-0.36
3. **Meaningful**: Every animation must relate to the industry
4. **Non-intrusive**: Text readability always wins over background
5. **Unified**: All cards share the same card structure, material, glass base
6. **Premium**: No neon, no game UI, no excessive particles, no flashy effects

## What NOT to do (binding)

- Do NOT increase opacity beyond approved ranges
- Do NOT add more particles beyond 3-8 per card
- Do NOT make animations faster than 8s period
- Do NOT add neon/glowing/bouncing effects
- Do NOT make cards look like game cards or cyberpunk
- Do NOT use AI-generated images as backgrounds
- Do NOT introduce Three.js or GLSL

## Baseline
This document serves as the visual baseline for all future P2 iterations.
Any visual change must be compared against this baseline.
